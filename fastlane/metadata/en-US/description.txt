Tellingly gives you one sharp, playful question a day: a moral dilemma, a secret habit, a hypothetical, or a preference you can answer in one tap.

The second you answer, you see the live distribution of how everyone else answered, how rare your choice was, and a short read on what that answer suggests about how you operate.

Your answers compound into a private pattern map. Each week, Tellingly turns that pattern into a shareable card: a rarity score and one specific verdict line that feels screenshot-worthy because it says the quiet part clearly.

Send a compatibility link to a friend and they answer the same set. You both see where your instincts line up and where they split, without building a feed, following anyone, or performing for a social graph.

No account required. No public profile. Just one sentence a day that tells you something true.
