Tellingly does not require login. The app asks one daily question, posts anonymous hashed answer events to Cloudflare D1 for aggregate distribution counts, and stores the user's pattern locally. Use the app normally by tapping any answer. Comparison links are created from the final screen and expire after 30 days. Tellingly Plus unlocks the full answer archive, trait trends, unlimited compatibility links, comparison history, and extra question packs. To trigger the paywall in this build, answer today's question, finish the round, create one free compatibility link, then tap Compare again. The paywall includes monthly and annual plans plus a visible Restore Purchases control. No third-party ads, camera, contacts, microphone, location, or social login are used in this build.
