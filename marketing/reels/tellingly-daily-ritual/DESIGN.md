# Tellingly Reel Design

## Style Prompt

Dark editorial mobile-native visual language with warm ivory type, charcoal panels, coral rarity accents, mint proof points, and crisp kinetic typography. It should feel like a private group-chat truth surfaced with product polish.

## Colors

- Background: `#101116`
- Panel: `#202229`
- Ivory text: `#f6f0df`
- Muted text: `#a6a094`
- Coral accent: `#ff6046`
- Mint accent: `#8de6c1`
- Gold accent: `#f8d66d`

## Typography

- Headlines: Georgia or a high-contrast serif.
- Interface/detail text: Inter or system sans-serif.

## Motion

- Fast vertical cuts.
- Percentage bars fill from left to right.
- Verdict line lands with a slight upward rise.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use astrology symbols.
- Do not use clinical/therapy language.
- Do not use soft pastel wellness styling.
- Do not imply ranking a person's worth.
