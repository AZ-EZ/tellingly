Tellingly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Tellingly is built to work without a public profile, social graph, or required account.

What We Collect

  • Daily answer choices, stored as aggregate counts for each question option.
  • A hashed anonymous device identifier used only to prevent one device from counting the same question repeatedly on the same day.
  • Comparison-link payloads when you explicitly create a link. These include the answered question IDs, answer labels, rarity score, and verdict line needed for your friend to compare.
  • Email address only if you join the launch or beta waitlist.

What Stays Local

Your longer-running pattern map and streak are stored in your browser or app local storage unless you choose to create a comparison link.

How We Use Data

  • To show live answer distributions and rarity scores.
  • To generate comparison links that expire after 30 days.
  • To contact beta or launch waitlist members who opted in.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run third-party ads in this version.
  • We do not require location, contacts, camera, microphone, or a social login.

Retention and Deletion

Comparison links expire after 30 days. Waitlist email deletion requests can be sent to support@tellingly.app. Aggregate answer counts may remain because they are not designed to identify a person.

Contact

Email support@tellingly.app. Replace this address with your active support mailbox before App Store submission.
